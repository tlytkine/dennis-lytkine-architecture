Our Services

What We Offer

Plan Review – Accelerated Approvals and Earlier Project Starts

Plan review is the critical first step to securing your building permit. At Private Provider SWFL, our expert plan review service confirms that your construction plans fully meet all applicable local and state building codes. We streamline and fast-track the entire review process so your project can advance more quickly.

When you choose Private Provider SWFL, local building departments are not required to perform the plan review themselves. As a result, municipalities must legally reduce standard permit fees and issue permits within 20 days or less. This allows you to start construction sooner and bypass the typical delays of conventional permitting processes.

Building Inspections – Keeping Your Project Moving Smoothly

Timely and reliable inspections are essential to maintaining project momentum. Our licensed inspectors perform thorough, code-compliant reviews in accordance with the Florida Building Code. If issues are identified, we go beyond simply noting deficiencies—we provide practical solutions and compliant alternatives to help you resolve them quickly and stay on schedule.

As your dedicated inspection partner, we handle direct communication with your local building department, ensuring they remain fully updated on your project's progress. Under Florida Statute 553.791, using third-party inspections entitles you to discounted permit fees, lowering your overall costs while reducing bureaucratic delays. With Private Provider SWFL, you benefit from simplified processes, faster approvals, and dependable on-time project completion.
