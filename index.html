Our Services

What We Offer

Architectural Design – From Concept to Construction

Great architecture starts with a clear vision. Our comprehensive architectural design services guide your project from initial concept through construction documentation. Dennis Lytkine brings extensive experience in space planning, interior architecture, and commercial design to every project.

Whether you're planning a new build or a renovation, we deliver designs that are both functional and aesthetically compelling. Our proficiency with Revit, AutoCAD, and modern design tools ensures precise documentation that contractors can build from with confidence, reducing costly change orders and construction delays.

Project Licensing – Ensuring Full Compliance

Navigating Florida's building codes and licensing requirements can be complex. Our project licensing services ensure that your construction project meets all local and state regulations from the start. As a licensed architect with NCARB certification, Dennis provides the professional stamp and oversight your project requires.

We work directly with local building departments and permitting authorities to streamline the approval process. Our deep understanding of Florida building codes means fewer revisions, faster approvals, and a smoother path to breaking ground on your project.

Construction Consulting – Expert Guidance at Every Stage

Construction projects involve countless decisions that impact quality, cost, and timeline. Our construction consulting services leverage years of hands-on experience to help you make informed decisions throughout your project. From material selection to contractor coordination, we provide the expert guidance you need.

As your consulting partner, we identify potential issues early, offer practical solutions, and help keep your project on track and within budget. Our goal is to ensure that the finished result matches the original design intent while meeting all applicable codes and standards.

Sustainable Design – Building for the Future

As a LEED Green Associate, Dennis brings a commitment to environmentally responsible design to every project. We integrate sustainable strategies that reduce energy consumption, minimize environmental impact, and create healthier spaces for occupants—all while maintaining design excellence and cost-effectiveness.

Why Choose Dennis Lytkine Architecture?

Dennis works closely with property owners, engineers, contractors, and local authorities to ensure that your project progresses smoothly, efficiently, and in full compliance with Florida's building codes. With credentials from the American Institute of Architects, NCARB certification, and LEED Green Associate status, you can trust that your project is in expert hands.

Our team has provided architectural and consulting services for a wide range of projects throughout Southwest Florida, including:

Commercial Buildings
Office Interiors
Residential Properties
Renovations & Additions

With deep local expertise, we are able to navigate the unique challenges of building in Southwest Florida, offering you a seamless experience and superior service at every stage of your project.
